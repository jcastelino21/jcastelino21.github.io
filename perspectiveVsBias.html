Overview

Perspective Bias
Perspective is a person's point of view Bias is the feeling of or against someone or something, usually in a way that is considered unfair
Every source has a perspective Not every source has a bias
To determine a sources perspective, you can find it by doing background research on the source creator To Determine a source's bias, you need to find specific words in the source that are either extremely positive or extremely negative

Once you know the perspective of a source, you may naturally expect that they demonstrate a particular kind of Bias, although not all sources will have a particular bias.

Perspective can be Influenced by:

  • Nationality
  • Occupation
  • Political Opinion
  • Cultural Background
  • Gender Identity
  • Religious Belief
  • Educational BAckground
